Animation

 

Throughout my creative journey, I have had the opportunity to explore various animation projects, allowing me to expand my skill set and delve deeper

into the captivating world of animation. One of my notable endeavors has been in the realm of hand-drawn animation, where I have dedicated my time

and passion to breathing life into characters and stories through meticulous frame-by-frame animation techniques.

 

One of my most recent and exciting endeavors involved the animation of a mesmerizing cat tail using Adobe Animate. Through this project, I delved into

into the nuances of character movement, timing, and fluidity, meticulously bringing each tail sway and flick to life. This experience not only allowed

me to showcase my technical proficiency but also ignited my passion for creating captivating and visually stunning animations.
